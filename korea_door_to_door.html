What is Door-to-Door?

Goodlugg's Door-to-Door service picks up your luggage from your current hotel or airport and delivers it directly to your next hotel — so you can explore Korea completely hands-free.

No heavy lifting. No taxi struggles. No waiting at the lobby. Just drop off before 9 AM and your bags will be waiting at your next hotel by 9 PM.
